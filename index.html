ABOUT OUR SCHOOL

Located 55 miles west of Komotini, Greece, the high school includes grades 9-12 with an enrollment of over 1750 students. As a diverse learning community of students, families, and professional educators, we respect both our differences and similarities in race, ethnicity, culture, language, religion, ideology, life choices, sexual orientation, socio-economic status, physical and mental differences, gende age, appearance, and marital status. Students, staff and families, work toward a climate of trust and respec. These are essential for an effective learning environment.Creating and promoting a positive environment for all to understand that doing so is everybody's responsibility.

Earth and sky, woods and fields, lakes and rivers, the mountain and the sea, are excellent schoolmasters, and teach some of us more than we can ever learn from books.

SERVICES

In our school you can also find:

Library

High School Library is committed to provide not only the resources to support the curriculum, but also educational, informational, and recreational materials.

Computer Center

Our computer learning center was launched in 1996 with the intent of teaching computer basic usage to adults who were just entering the technology world.

Career Center

Anatolia and Career Center works in conjunction with the Counseling Office to provide information that helps students relate school subjects to the world of work.

Schoolarships

We offer a variety of scholarships to new and continuing students.

Activity Classes

Students who love music, technology, or journalism will find that full participation is enhanced by after-school and extra curricular experiences.

Health Service

Our Health Center is a safe, confidential place where students from Pludynamo High School can receive medical, nutritional services and mental health care.
